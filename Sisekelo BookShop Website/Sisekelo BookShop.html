About Sisekelo BookShop


This is a 100% Swazi and youth owned company and our goal is to be a leading supplier office stationery, school stationery, cleaning material and many more.

With the establishment of the company, we are initially to specialize in office stationery and we are hoping to grow over the yearsd and employ more young people as job opportunities arises in our company.

We believe in creating a long-term relationship with our clients and stakeholders. This term will make them feel very welcomed when they are interacting with the members of the company. As we all know that a happy customer means a happy company.
